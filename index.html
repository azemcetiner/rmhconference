Racialized Maternal Health Conference

Join our 2019 Conference!

This free conference aims to bring together clinicians, students, community members, policy makers, and researchers, for much needed discussions on racialized maternal health.

The theme of this year's conference is: Politics of the Black Pregnant Body.

Through talks, panel discussions, and poster presentations, this conference aims to educate, inspire dialogue, and create opportunities for networking and change within the Canadian maternal health system.
